General Instructions:

  • You have two options available under this category of practice contest:
    • Option - 1: Cook your own problemset by selecting problems from past CodeChef Cook-Offs and challenge your friends to solve it.
    • Option - 2: Solve a problemset cooked by one of your fellow coder.
  • Each contest (or a problemset) can consist of any number of problems.
  • Each contest (or a problemset) can be of any time length. The length of the contest comes under the decision of the cook.
  • You can create/participate in only one practice contest (of either of the three categories) at any instant of time.
  • All other instructions and rules of a CodeChef Cook-Off are applicable in the practice contest.
  • We believe that it is alright to refer to tutorials, books and other materials, learn a concept and then apply the same to solve a problem during a practice contest. But it is not alright to copy other people's solutions or seek other people's help to solve a problem without understanding it.
  • Since, v1.0 of CodeChef API does not provide us with the option to 'submit' problems on behalf of users, please have a look at the following video to understand how one is required to submit her/his solution to a problem.
